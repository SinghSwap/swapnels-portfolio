## Projects & Case Studies

### 1. Sunday Basket *(personal product)*
AI-powered weekly meal-planning assistant that cuts grocery costs and removes the effort of deciding what to cook. The real problem was ~1 hour of weekly planning, not shopping. Sunday-morning automation generates a 7-day plan optimized for ingredient reuse, consolidates one categorized grocery list, and emails it before shopping — cutting planning to under 5 minutes. (Make.com + AI.) Full case study covers JTBD, MVP scope, user flow, architecture, success metrics, and a "if I turned it into a product" section.

### 2. AI-Powered Job Search Engine *(personal product)*
ATS-integrated engine (Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby APIs) that fetches PM roles automatically and scores them against a resume with AI/embeddings. Cut job-search effort from hours to minutes. (Python, OpenAI.)

### 3. Personal Finance AI App *(mobile product)*
iOS app concept that classifies spending and gives AI-powered need-vs-want insights. (SwiftUI, OpenAI.)

### 4. PM Revision *(learning product)*
Offline web app organizing PM frameworks, metrics, and case studies into one local-storage knowledge system. (HTML/CSS/JS.)

### 5. WhatsApp Business Search *(product research)*
User research + product proposal for context-aware information retrieval inside business chats. TAM/SAM/SOM, interviews, journey mapping, PRDs, wireframes.

### 6. Swiggy Instamart — Variant Selection Friction *(product teardown)*
Growth-PM teardown of the grocery add-to-cart funnel. Thesis: the variant picker optimizes for discount/AOV exposure at the moment of purchase intent instead of completion — auto-selecting sold-out packs, hiding the sold-out state, and rendering the correct default last. Includes root-cause analysis, RICE prioritization across 5 interventions, a 6-test experiment plan, and a recommendation to ship availability-first defaulting + an unmissable sold-out state, gated on revenue-per-session not AOV.
